About

SMM Pro Desktop is a desktop application that helps content creators, social media managers, and businesses manage multiple social media accounts efficiently. Connect your accounts once and schedule posts, bulk publish content, and track your activity — all from a single interface on your computer.

Your credentials are stored encrypted (AES-256-GCM) in your own database. We do not operate centralized servers that store your personal data.

Features
🔗

Connect Accounts via OAuth

Securely connect your social media accounts using official OAuth 2.0 flows. Tokens are stored encrypted on your device.

📅

Schedule Posts

Schedule content to be published at a specific date and time. The app automatically dispatches posts when they are due.

📤

Bulk Publishing

Publish the same post to multiple accounts and platforms simultaneously. Supports text, images, and video content.

🔒

Secure & Private

All OAuth tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Your data stays in your own Supabase database instance.

How It Works
  1. Download and install SMM Pro Desktop on your computer
  2. Connect your social media accounts using the secure OAuth login flow
  3. Create your post content and select which accounts to publish to
  4. Choose to publish immediately or schedule for a later date and time
  5. The app queues and publishes your content automatically via the worker service

TikTok Integration

SMM Pro Desktop integrates with TikTok using the official TikTok for Developers API. The following permissions are requested:

ScopePurpose
user.info.basicRetrieve your TikTok display name and user ID to identify your connected account in the app
video.uploadUpload video files you select from your computer to TikTok's servers
video.publishPublish uploaded videos to your TikTok profile when you initiate a post

TikTok data is used solely for the purposes above and is never shared with third parties.
